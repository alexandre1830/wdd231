Site Plan

Site name

Aquele com o Brownie

Optional domain availability: aquelecomobrownie.com

Site Purpose

The site provides information about the brand, the products with illustrative photos, the address with an embedded map and a form to get more information and order the brownies.

Scenarios

  • What are the options of brownies I can purchase?
  • Where can I get my order?
  • How can I reach out to purchase the brownies?

Color scheme

Primary color: #3f5d16 (dark green) used for main headings and background color for highlighted parts.

Secondary color: #729e35 (light green) used for subheadings and some headings with dark green background.

Black: used for general text with white background.

White: used as background for most of the site and for some text with different background colors.

Fonts

Sour Gummy: headings and highlights

Google Sans: overall text
